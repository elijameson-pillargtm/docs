# Blueprint Assessment

The PILLAR Blueprint Assessment is a free, comprehensive GTM maturity evaluation. It measures your go-to-market operations across 5 pillars, 27 categories, and 142 questions — producing a detailed report with actionable insights.

<Card title="Take the Assessment" icon="arrow-right" href="https://pillargtm.com/blueprint">
  Complete the Blueprint Assessment and receive your GTM maturity report
</Card>

## What It Measures

The assessment evaluates your organization across the 5 pillars of GTM operational excellence:

| Pillar | Categories | Questions | What It Evaluates |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Strategy** | 5 | 12 | ICP definition, market positioning, competitive differentiation, pricing strategy, GTM planning |
| **People** | 5 | 13 | Org structure, role clarity, hiring practices, enablement programs, leadership alignment |
| **Process** | 7 | 20 | Pipeline management, deal qualification, forecasting, renewal workflows, expansion motions, territory planning, cross-functional alignment |
| **Systems** | 5 | 14 | CRM hygiene, tech stack integration, data governance, automation maturity, reporting infrastructure |
| **Metrics** | 5 | 13 | KPI framework, attribution modeling, forecasting accuracy, unit economics tracking, board reporting |

## How Scoring Works

Each question is scored on a 3-point scale:

| Response | Score | Meaning |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Not in place | 0 | This capability does not exist in your organization |
| Partially in place | 1 | Some elements exist but are inconsistent or incomplete |
| Fully in place | 2 | This capability is well-established and consistently executed |

**Pillar scores** are calculated as the percentage of maximum possible points within that pillar. For example, if Strategy has 12 questions (max 24 points) and you score 18, your Strategy pillar score is 75%.

**Overall score** is the weighted average across all 5 pillars.

## Maturity Tiers

Your overall score maps to one of four maturity tiers:

| Tier | Score Range | Description |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **Foundational** | 0-39% | Core GTM processes are missing or ad hoc. Significant structural gaps exist across most pillars. Immediate foundational work is needed. |
| **Emerging** | 40-59% | Basic GTM structure exists but execution is inconsistent. Key processes are partially defined. Focus areas are becoming clear. |
| **Scaling** | 60-79% | GTM operations are established and repeatable. Most processes are documented and followed. Optimization opportunities remain. |
| **Optimized** | 80-100% | GTM operations are data-driven and continuously improving. Processes are automated where possible. The organization operates as a revenue machine. |

## Your Report

After completing the assessment, you receive a comprehensive report including:

1. **Overall maturity tier** with composite score
2. **Per-pillar scores** with visual breakdowns
3. **Strengths analysis** — what your organization does well, with specific category-level detail
4. **Gap analysis** — where the biggest opportunities for improvement exist
5. **Next steps** — concrete actions to take in each pillar
6. **Maturity roadmap** — a 90-day improvement plan

Reports are generated using AI analysis calibrated against the actual scoring data, ensuring every insight is grounded in your specific responses.
