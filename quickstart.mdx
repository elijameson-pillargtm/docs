# Getting Started

PILLAR connects to your CRM, enriches every account with district intelligence, and starts generating health scores, risk signals, and revenue insights automatically. This guide walks you through setup.

## Step 1: Accept Your Invitation

Your organization admin will send you an invite via email. Click the link to create your account. PILLAR uses Supabase Auth with Row-Level Security, so your data is isolated to your organization from the moment you sign in.

<Card title="Authentication Details" icon="lock" href="/api/authentication">
  Learn more about auth flows, user roles, and RLS policies
</Card>

## Step 2: Connect Your CRM

Navigate to **Settings > Connectors** and authenticate with your CRM. PILLAR supports:

| CRM | Sync Frequency | Objects Synced |
|-----|---------------|----------------|
| Salesforce | Every 5 minutes | Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, Leads |
| HubSpot | Every 5 minutes | Companies, Contacts, Deals, Tickets |

The initial sync typically completes in 5-15 minutes depending on the size of your CRM. You can monitor progress in the Connectors dashboard.

<Card title="Connectors API" icon="plug" href="/api/connectors">
  Technical details on connector configuration and sync status
</Card>

## Step 3: Connect Email and Calendar

PILLAR ingests email metadata and calendar events to power activity-based signals. Navigate to **Settings > Integrations** and connect:

- **Google Workspace** — email send/receive metadata, calendar events
- **Microsoft 365** — email and calendar (coming soon)

PILLAR never reads email body content. Only metadata (sender, recipient, timestamp, subject line) is ingested for activity scoring.

Navigate to **Settings > Scoring** to adjust category weights. Changes take effect on the next scoring cycle (within 5 minutes).

## Step 5: Review Your Dashboard

Once your CRM sync completes, your dashboard populates automatically:

- **Pipeline Overview** — total pipeline value, stage distribution, hygiene scores
- **Account Health** — top accounts by health score, accounts needing attention
- **Signals** — active signals requiring action, organized by severity
- **Territory View** — geographic distribution and territory equity metrics

## Step 6: Understand Your Signals

PILLAR generates signals when scoring outputs cross configurable thresholds or when external events are detected. Signals are the primary action-driving mechanism in the platform.

## What Happens Next

After initial setup, PILLAR continuously:

1. **Syncs** CRM data every 5 minutes
2. **Enriches** accounts with Starbridge district intelligence
3. **Scores** every account against 93+ rules
4. **Generates signals** when thresholds are crossed
5. **Routes notifications** to Slack and email
6. **Updates dashboards** with real-time insights

Your team can start acting on signals immediately. No manual data entry required.
