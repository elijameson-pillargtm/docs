# Changelog

All notable changes to the PILLAR platform are documented here. Releases follow semantic versioning.

---

## v3.1.0 — March 2026

**Blueprint Assessment MVP**

- Launched the public Blueprint GTM Maturity Assessment at [pillargtm.com/blueprint](https://pillargtm.com/blueprint)
- 142-question assessment across 5 pillars and 27 categories
- AI-generated strengths and gaps analysis with consultant-grade depth
- PDF report generation with full pillar breakdown
- Lead capture gate with automatic email notifications
- Admin dashboard for managing submissions at [app.pillargtm.com/admin/blueprint](https://app.pillargtm.com/admin/blueprint)
- Maturity tier classification: Foundational, Emerging, Scaling, Optimized

---

## v3.0.0 — February 2026

**Scoring Engine v3 and Signal Taxonomy**

- Expanded scoring engine to 93+ rules across 8 categories
- Added configurable weights per organization via `org_scoring_profiles`
- New scoring categories: Expansion Readiness, Territory Equity, Economics Rules
- Signal Taxonomy v3: 8 signal families, 30+ signal types
- 7-state signal lifecycle with severity-based routing
- Slack real-time signal delivery and weekly email digest
- Starbridge district intelligence integration with 120+ buyer attributes

---

## v2.5.0 — January 2026

**Revenue Economics and Territory Management**

- Revenue Economics data model: CAC, LTV, magic number, payback period
- Territory management with geographic and named-account assignments
- Territory equity scoring to identify coverage imbalances
- Economics-based signals for unit economics anomalies

---

## v2.0.0 — December 2025

**AI Intelligence Layer**

- AI-powered recommended actions on every signal
- Natural language signal summaries
- Predictive churn modeling using renewal risk scores
- AI Intelligence data model with prompt history and confidence scores
- Plays engine: automated sequences triggered by signal combinations

---

## v1.5.0 — November 2025

**CRM Integrations and Real-Time Sync**

- Salesforce bidirectional sync (every 5 minutes)
- HubSpot bidirectional sync (every 5 minutes)
- Google Workspace email metadata and calendar event ingestion
- Activity tracking and engagement scoring
- Pipeline hygiene scoring with stage-level analysis

## v1.0.0 — October 2025

**Initial Platform Launch**

- Core data model: Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, Leads
